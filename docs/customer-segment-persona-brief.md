# Wiser Properties - Customer Segment & Persona Brief

## Overview

Wiser Properties serves three distinct customer segments with different needs, motivations, and user journeys. This document outlines each segment with detailed personas to guide product development, marketing messaging, and sales strategy.

---

## Customer Segment 1: Real Estate Developers (Primary)

### Segment Overview
**Priority**: Primary (launch focus)  
**Market**: Thailand & Malaysia  
**Size**: Mid to large developers with 10+ units  
**Revenue Model**: Subscription + credit-based promotions

### Why This Segment First
- Direct revenue source (subscription fees)
- Clear pain point (expensive traditional launches)
- Measurable ROI (inventory turnover)
- Platform built for their needs (developer dashboard)

---

### Persona 1: Mid-Market Developer (Primary Persona)

**Name**: Somchai Tan  
**Title**: Sales & Marketing Director  
**Company**: Tan Development Group  
**Location**: Bangkok, Thailand  
**Company Size**: 50-200 employees  
**Properties**: 3-5 active projects, 50-200 units per project

#### Demographics
- Age: 45-55
- Education: Bachelor's in Business/Engineering
- Experience: 15+ years in real estate
- Language: Fluent Thai, conversational English

#### Psychographics
- **Goals**: Move unsold inventory faster, reduce marketing costs, reach qualified buyers
- **Challenges**: 
  - High cost of traditional launches (150k+ MYR per event)
  - Difficulty reaching qualified prospects
  - Competition from other developers
  - Need to differentiate properties
- **Pain Points**:
  - Hotel launch events are expensive and don't guarantee sales
  - Traditional property portals are cluttered and agent-focused
  - Hard to showcase property quality online
  - Commission-based models eat into margins
- **Motivations**:
  - Cost efficiency (save on launch events)
  - Better lead quality
  - Faster sales cycles
  - Modern, tech-forward image

#### Behavior Patterns
- **Decision Making**: 
  - Evaluates ROI carefully
  - Prefers predictable costs over variable commissions
  - Needs to see platform before committing
  - Consults with team before signing
- **Technology Comfort**: 
  - Comfortable with basic tech (email, Excel, property management systems)
  - Not highly technical but open to new tools
  - Prefers simple, intuitive interfaces
- **Communication Style**:
  - Prefers in-person or video calls for important decisions
  - Responds well to case studies and ROI examples
  - Values relationship building
  - Needs Thai language support

#### Information Needs
- Platform demo/walkthrough
- Pricing transparency
- Success stories from similar developers
- Technical support availability
- Integration with existing systems

#### User Journey
1. **Awareness**: Hears about platform from industry event, referral, or online search
2. **Interest**: Reviews website, requests demo, compares to traditional launch costs
3. **Evaluation**: Tests platform, uploads sample property, reviews credit system
4. **Decision**: Negotiates subscription terms, signs up for trial period
5. **Onboarding**: Receives training, uploads properties, sets up promotions
6. **Usage**: Manages properties, uses credits for featured/discount listings, tracks leads
7. **Retention**: Sees results, renews subscription, expands usage

#### Messaging That Resonates
- "Replace your 150k MYR hotel launch with a subscription platform"
- "Reach qualified buyers in our database"
- "Showcase your properties with immersive 3D tours"
- "Pay only for promotions when you need them"

## Customer Segment 2: Real Estate Agents (Secondary - Future)

### Segment Overview
**Priority**: Secondary (post-launch expansion)  
**Market**: Thailand & Malaysia  
**Size**: Individual agents and small agencies  
**Revenue Model**: Subscription + credit-based promotions

### Why This Segment Second
- Larger market size but lower individual value
- Different needs (secondary market, cheaper 3D solution)
- Requires phone-based 3D walkthrough (in development)
- Regulatory considerations (Malaysia requires agency license)

#### Psychographics

  - Can't visit properties easily (geographic distance)
  - Hard to compare properties across different developers
  - Language barriers
  - Need to understand market and pricing
- **Pain Points**:
  - Limited property information online
  - Can't experience properties before visiting
  - Hard to compare options
  - Unclear pricing and discounts
- **Motivations**:
  - Investment returns
  - Lifestyle (holiday home, retirement)
  - Portfolio diversification
  - Good deals and discounts

#### Behavior Patterns
- **Decision Making**:
  - Research-heavy
  - Compares multiple options
  - Values transparency
  - Needs detailed information
- **Technology Comfort**:
  - High tech comfort
  - Uses multiple devices
  - Expects modern, polished interfaces
- **Communication Style**:
  - Direct, professional
  - English preferred
  - Values quick responses
  - Data-driven

#### Information Needs
- Property details (size, price, location, amenities)
- 3D walkthroughs (can't visit in person)
- Comparison tools (evaluate options)
- Pricing transparency
- Developer information
- Market context

#### User Journey
1. **Awareness**: Finds platform through search, referral, or developer marketing
2. **Interest**: Searches for properties in target location/price range
3. **Exploration**: Views 3D walkthroughs, compares properties, saves favorites
4. **Evaluation**: Uses comparison tool, reviews details, checks pricing
5. **Engagement**: Contacts developer through platform, schedules visit
6. **Decision**: Makes purchase decision (off-platform)
7. **Advocacy**: Shares platform with others if positive experience

#### Messaging That Resonates
- "Explore properties in 3D from anywhere in the world"
- "Compare luxury properties side-by-side"
- "Find the best deals on new developments"
- "Make informed decisions before visiting"

## Key Insights for Product & Marketing

### For Developers
- **Value**: Cost savings + better targeting
- **Messaging**: Replace expensive launches, reach qualified buyers
- **Features**: Easy upload, credit system, analytics
- **Support**: Thai/Malay language, training, onboarding

### For Agents
- **Value**: Affordable 3D solution + listing platform
- **Messaging**: Phone-based 3D, lower costs than portals
- **Features**: Phone 3D capture, easy listing, credit system
- **Support**: Mobile-friendly, simple interface, training

### For Prospects
- **Value**: Immersive exploration + comparison tools
- **Messaging**: 3D tours, side-by-side comparison, best deals
- **Features**: Comparison tool, 3D walkthroughs, favorites, search
- **Support**: Multi-language, clear information, responsive design
