# Wiser Properties - Positioning Memo

## Executive Summary

Wiser Properties is a developer-centric property comparison platform that helps real estate developers in Thailand and Malaysia sell unsold inventory faster through immersive 3D walkthroughs, intelligent comparison tools, and a cost-effective subscription model that replaces expensive traditional launch events.

---

## Market Position

### Primary Market Focus
- **Geographic**: Thailand (Bangkok, Phuket, Pattaya) and Malaysia (KL, Penang)
- **Market Type**: Primary market (new developments) - NOT secondary/resale market
- **Property Segments**: Luxury, mid-market, and regular developments
- **Competitive Gap**: No existing platform focuses exclusively on primary market with immersive comparison features

### Current Market Reality
- **Problem**: Developers have built massive luxury condominium inventory that isn't selling
- **Traditional Solution Cost**: 150,000-160,000 MYR for hotel launches (ballroom rental, advertising, agency fees)
- **Market Behavior**: Most property portals (iProperty, PropertyGuru, FazWaz) focus 90% on secondary market with agent listings
- **Developer Pain Point**: Need to move inventory without massive upfront costs and with better prospect targeting

---

## Value Proposition

### For Developers

**"Sell your unsold inventory at a fraction of traditional launch costs with better prospect targeting"**

**Key Benefits:**
1. **Cost Efficiency**: Subscription model replaces expensive hotel launches (save 90%+ on launch costs)
2. **Prospect Database**: Access to pre-qualified buyer database instead of cold outreach
3. **Immersive Experience**: 3D Matterport walkthroughs allow prospects to experience properties before visiting
4. **Comparison Tools**: Side-by-side property comparison helps buyers make faster decisions
5. **Credit-Based Promotion**: Pay only for featured/discount listings when needed (not per-listing fees)
6. **Developer Control**: Direct platform access without mandatory real estate agency intermediaries

### For Property Buyers/Prospects

**"Compare luxury properties side-by-side with immersive 3D tours before visiting"**

**Key Benefits:**
1. **Immersive Exploration**: 360° Matterport walkthroughs from anywhere
2. **Intelligent Comparison**: Side-by-side comparison of multiple properties across 25+ attributes
3. **Aerial Views**: Drone footage showing neighborhood context
4. **Transparent Pricing**: Best prices with potential additional developer discounts
5. **Time Savings**: Pre-qualify properties before scheduling show unit visits

---

## Differentiation Strategy

### What Makes Wiser Properties Different

1. **Primary Market Focus**
   - Only platform exclusively targeting developers with unsold inventory
   - NOT an agent marketplace (agents are secondary, future feature)
   - Direct developer-to-buyer relationship

2. **Immersive Technology Stack**
   - Matterport 3D walkthroughs (not just static images)
   - Aerial drone videos for neighborhood context
   - Phone-based 3D solution for agents (cheaper alternative to Matterport)
   - Planned: CG renderings for off-plan properties

3. **Comparison-First Design**
   - Built-in comparison tool (not just listing)
   - Side-by-side view of 25+ property attributes
   - Visual comparison of interior views, facilities, and locations

4. **Subscription + Credit Model**
   - No commission fees (unlike traditional agencies)
   - Subscription with credit system for promotions
   - Featured and discount property credits
   - Transparent, predictable costs

5. **Developer-Centric Features**
   - Direct property upload and management
   - Credit-based promotional system
   - Analytics and prospect tracking (planned)
   - Chat feature to keep conversations on-platform (planned)
