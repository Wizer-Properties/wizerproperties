## Analytics: What We're Tracking and Why

We've set up comprehensive analytics tracking across three platforms to give you complete visibility into user behavior and business performance:

### **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)**
**What we track:**
- Property views (which properties get the most interest)
- Property favorites and comparisons (buyer intent signals)
- Search behavior (what buyers are looking for)
- User authentication events (signups and logins)
- Contact form submissions and viewing schedules

**Why it matters:**
- Identify your most popular properties to prioritize marketing
- Understand buyer search patterns to optimize property listings
- Track conversion funnel from property view to contact
- Measure which marketing channels drive quality leads

### **PostHog (Product Analytics)**
**What we track:**
- Detailed user journeys through your platform
- Feature usage (comparison tool, favorites, search filters)
- User identification for logged-in users
- Session recordings and user behavior patterns

**Why it matters:**
- See exactly how buyers navigate your platform
- Identify UX friction points that might be losing conversions
- Understand which features drive engagement
- Make data-driven decisions on product improvements

### **Meta Pixel (Facebook/Instagram)**
**What we track:**
- Property views and interactions
- Lead generation events (contact forms, schedules)
- User signups and registrations

**Why it matters:**
- Retarget website visitors with relevant property ads
- Build custom audiences for Facebook/Instagram campaigns
- Measure ROI on social media advertising spend
- Optimize ad campaigns based on actual conversions

### **Key Events We're Capturing:**

1. **Property View** - Every time someone views a property detail page
   - *Why:* Shows which properties generate interest

2. **Property Favorite** - When users save properties to their favorites
   - *Why:* Strong buying intent signal

3. **Property Comparison** - When users compare multiple properties
   - *Why:* Indicates serious evaluation phase

4. **Contact Form Submission** - When buyers request more information
   - *Why:* Direct lead generation metric

5. **Schedule Viewing** - When buyers book property viewings
   - *Why:* Highest intent action - these are your hottest leads

6. **User Signup/Login** - Account creation and authentication
   - *Why:* Tracks user engagement and repeat visitors

All tracking is privacy-compliant and follows best practices. We only track identified users when they explicitly log in, and all data is anonymized for non-authenticated visitors.

## CRM Structure: How We're Organizing Your Leads

We've configured your Zoho CRM to match your real estate sales process. Here's how it's structured:

### **Lead Capture Flow:**

**From Website → CRM:**
1. **Contact Form Submission** → Creates a Lead in Zoho
2. **Schedule Viewing Form** → Creates both a Contact and a Deal

All leads automatically include:
- Property details (ID, name, building, location)
- Property specifications (bedrooms, bathrooms, size, price)
- Buyer information (name, email, phone)
- Source tracking (which page/form they came from)

### **Deal Pipeline Stages:**

We've set up an 8-stage pipeline that matches your sales process:

1. **New Inquiry** (10% probability) - Just received from platform
2. **Qualified** (20%) - Budget and timeline confirmed
3. **Viewing Scheduled** (30%) - Property viewing booked
4. **Viewing Completed** (40%) - Buyer has seen the property
5. **Negotiation** (60%) - Price/terms discussion
6. **Offer Submitted** (70%) - Formal offer made
7. **Contract Signed** (90%) - Deposit paid, contract executed
8. **Closed Won** (100%) - Sale completed

**Why this structure:**
- Clear visibility into where each deal stands
- Automatic probability scoring for revenue forecasting
- Easy identification of bottlenecks in your sales process
- Standardized process for your team

### **Custom Fields We're Tracking:**

**For Leads/Contacts:**
- Property preferences (type, location, budget range)
- Bedrooms/bathrooms requirements
- Investment purpose (primary residence, investment, rental)
- Move-in timeline
- Nationality and language preference

**For Deals:**
- Property details (ID, URL, building name, location)
- Property specifications (size, bedrooms, bathrooms, floor)
- Building status (pre-sale, under construction, completed)
- Viewing information (scheduled time, status)
- Deal value and stage

**Why these fields:**
- Qualify leads faster (budget, timeline, preferences)
- Personalize follow-up communications
- Track which properties generate the most interest
- Measure viewing conversion rates
- Identify high-value opportunities

### **Automated Workflows:**

We've set up automation to save you time:

1. **New Lead Notification** - Instant email when inquiry comes in
2. **Viewing Confirmation** - Automatic confirmation emails to buyers
3. **Follow-Up Reminders** - Tasks created for post-viewing follow-ups
4. **Stale Lead Alerts** - Notifications when leads go cold (>7 days)

**Why automation:**
- Faster response times = higher conversion rates
- Never miss a follow-up opportunity
- Consistent communication with all leads
- Your team can focus on closing deals, not admin

### **Lead Scoring:**

Leads are automatically scored based on:
- **Positive signals:** Scheduled viewing (+30), completed viewing (+40), high budget (+20), immediate timeline (+25)
- **Negative signals:** No activity for 14+ days (-20), no-show at viewing (-30)

**Score tiers:**
- **Hot (80+)** - Prioritize immediately
- **Warm (50-79)** - Standard follow-up
- **Cool (20-49)** - Nurture sequence
- **Cold (<20)** - Re-engagement or archive

**Why scoring:**
- Focus your team's time on highest-value opportunities
- Identify which leads need immediate attention
- Automatically prioritize your pipeline
