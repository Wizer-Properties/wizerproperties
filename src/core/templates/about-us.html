About WiSER Properties

Connecting global investors with Thailand’s most trusted developments

We transform how property teams and international buyers discover, evaluate, and close deals across Thailand. Our marketplace blends verified data, immersive media, and personalised support so every decision feels confident.

Market intelligence

From launch calendars to price history, WiSER curates data directly from developers so buyers can understand supply, incentives, and after-sales performance at a glance.

Unified collaboration

Agents, developers, and marketing teams coordinate through a single workspace—sharing listings, reels, analytics, and buyer updates without spreadsheets or email chains.

Experience-first buying

International buyers tour projects digitally, compare units, and book consultations securely—enjoying concierge-level support in their language and time zone.

Our mission

To make cross-border property transactions intuitive, transparent, and human. WiSER removes friction from discovery through handover, empowering teams to scale internationally while retaining the bespoke service buyers expect.

  • Verified, tokenised listing data keeps every team member aligned on pricing, incentives, and availability.
  • Immersive media pipelines—from Splide reels to AI descriptions—bring developments to life in any channel.
  • Guided workflows ensure compliance, documentation, and communications scale without compromising trust.
