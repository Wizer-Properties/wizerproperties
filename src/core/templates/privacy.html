Privacy & Security

How WiSER protects your data

We take your trust seriously. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and the controls you have over your information when using WiSER Properties.

1. Information we collect

We collect information in three primary ways:

  • Information you provide when creating an account, saving listings, booking consultations, or contacting support. This may include your name, email, phone number, investment preferences, and communication history.
  • Usage information such as pages visited, search filters, and device metadata that help us improve performance and relevance.
  • Partner and public data used to verify listings, pricing, and developer credentials to keep marketplace content accurate.

2. How we use your information

We process your data to:

  • Deliver core functionality such as personalised search, saved listings, and booking flows.
  • Connect you with relevant developers, agents, and underwriting partners when you request project information.
  • Send product updates, market insights, and compliance notices you opt into.
  • Maintain platform reliability, prevent fraud, and meet regulatory requirements in the markets we operate.

We never sell your personal information. Any vendor partners we engage must meet WiSER’s security standards and sign data processing agreements.

3. Protecting your data

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2+ and stored using AES-256 encryption at rest.
  • Access to production systems is gated behind role-based controls, hardware security keys, and continuous monitoring.
  • We conduct periodic penetration tests, vendor risk reviews, and incident response simulations.
  • Our infrastructure is hosted with providers that maintain ISO 27001, SOC 2, and GDPR compliance.

4. Your privacy controls

You can manage your information at any time:

  • Update profile details, communication preferences, and saved searches directly in your account.
  • Request export or deletion of your data by contacting privacy@wizerproperties.com.
  • Opt out of marketing updates via unsubscribe links embedded in every campaign.

5. Updates to this policy

We review this policy regularly to reflect product enhancements or regulatory changes. When we make updates, we will:

  • Publish the new effective date below.
  • Notify active users via email or in-app messaging for material changes.
  • Maintain a changelog so you can track what changed and why.

Last updated: 10 November 2025

If you have questions about this policy or need assistance exercising your rights, our privacy team is here to help.
