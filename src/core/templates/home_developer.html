Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Wizer Properties for developers

How is this different from PropertyGuru or iProperty?

Wizer Properties is built exclusively for developers selling primary market inventory. Unlike traditional portals that focus on agents and secondary market, we provide direct developer-to-buyer relationships, immersive 3D comparison tools, and a subscription model instead of per-listing fees or commissions. We're the only platform that helps buyers compare properties side-by-side with 25+ attributes.

Do I need Matterport cameras?

No, you don't need expensive Matterport cameras. You can use existing Matterport tours if you have them, or use our phone-based 3D walkthrough solution that creates immersive tours from your smartphone photos at a fraction of the cost. This makes 3D tours accessible for all developers, not just those with large marketing budgets.

How do I get qualified leads?

Our platform attracts qualified buyers through SEO, content marketing, and our unique comparison tools. When buyers use our platform to compare properties, you get direct inquiries. We also provide analytics to track which properties get the most views and inquiries. The comparison tool helps buyers make faster decisions, which means faster sales for you.

What's the credit system?

Credits are used to promote your properties. You get 100 free credits when you sign up. Use 3 credits to feature a property on the homepage for a week, or 3 credits to create a flash sale. Top up credits as needed—no monthly subscription fees until you make your first sale. This gives you complete control over your marketing spend.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, absolutely. There's no long-term contract. You can cancel your subscription at any time. Your properties will remain listed, but you won't be able to use credits for promotions without an active subscription. We believe in earning your business every month, not locking you into contracts.

How much can I save compared to traditional launches?

Traditional hotel launches cost 1.2 Million THB per event with no guaranteed sales. With Wizer Properties, you pay a monthly subscription (free until first sale) plus credits for promotions (3 credits per featured listing or flash sale). Even with heavy promotion, you'll save 90% compared to traditional launch costs. Plus, your properties stay listed 24/7, not just during launch events.
